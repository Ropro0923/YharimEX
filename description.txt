This mod adds Yharim, the Godseeker, the final boss of the Calamity Mod.

This mod is unofficial and is not affiliated with the Calamity Mod team. It is a port of the Yharim EX fight from 1.4.3 to 1.4.4, with some changes to the fight mechanics and visuals.

Source Code: https://github.com/Ropro0923/YharimEX
